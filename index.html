Motivate Developer Resources

We here at Motivate love data, so we built this page for all the other transit and government data lovers out there that are interested in bike share system data. Its a centralized place for all of our data feeds, trip histories, our Data Use Policy, and links to some useful third-party bike share related libraries and tools.

Technical Details

  • Our feeds generally update once every minute. Please don't hit any one of them more often than that.
  • Older feeds are in XML format, the newer ones are in JSON format.
  • Each feed has an internal timestamp, use it to determine when the data was generated. The XML feeds use 13-digit unix timestamps including miliseconds.
  • Every public station is listed in the feed with its lat/lon values. Sometimes an address, landmark, or city is also included.
  • The feed includes how many bicycles and docks are available at each station.
  • Each station has an unique ID (id) that should never change.
