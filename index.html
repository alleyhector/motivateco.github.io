Motivate Developer Resources

We here at Motivate love data. developers.motivateco.com is for transit and government data lovers interested in bike share. You'll find links to our data feeds, trip histories, our Data Use Policy, and some useful third-party libraries and tools.

Technical Details

  • Our feeds generally update once every minute. Please don't hit any one of them more often than that.
  • Some feeds are in XML format, others are in JSON format.
  • Each feed has a timestamp, use it to determine when the data was generated. Some feeds use 13-digit unix timestamps including miliseconds, other use YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS [AM|PM].
  • Every public station is listed in the feed with its lat/lon values. Sometimes an address, landmark, or city is also included.
  • The feed includes how many bicycles and docks are available at each station.
  • Each station has an unique id that should never change.

Motivate does not support any libraries, code, or data contained in any of the links in this section.
